Our History

Four decades of excellence

1985
Founding of Neo University
Established by the Lumina Foundation with 120 founding students across three departments.
1995
First Regional Championship
Debate and Science teams win back-to-back regional titles, establishing Lumina's academic reputation.
2008
Campus Expansion
New main building, covered courts, and library wing open — capacity doubles to over 1,000 students.
2019
Digital Transformation
Launch of the Student Portal and Learning Management System, modernizing student services.
2024
National Excellence Award
Recognized as one of the top 10 performing school organizations in the national convention.
