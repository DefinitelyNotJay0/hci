Housing & Residential Life

Choose from a variety of housing options designed to fit your lifestyle and needs

Freshman Residence Halls

Traditional dormitories with double rooms, shared bathrooms, and common areas. Perfect for making new friends.

  • Resident Advisors
  • Study Lounges
  • 24/7 Security
  • Dining Hall Access

Upperclass Apartments

Suite-style living with private bedrooms, shared kitchens, and living spaces for more independence.

  • Full Kitchen
  • Private Bathrooms
  • Living Room
  • On-Campus Location

Graduate & Family Housing

1-3 bedroom apartments for graduate students, couples, and families with children.

  • Pet Friendly
  • Parking Included
  • Playground
  • Community Center
ℹ️
Housing Application

All first-year students are required to live on campus. Housing applications are available after admission acceptance. Applications are processed on a first-come, first-served basis, so apply early to secure your preferred housing option.
