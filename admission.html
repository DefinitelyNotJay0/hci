Admissions

Begin your journey to excellence — we're here to guide you every step of the way.

Application Requirements

Here's everything you need to submit with your application

High School Transcript

Official transcripts showing all coursework and grades.

Standardized Tests

SAT or ACT scores (optional for 2026-2027).

Letters of Recommendation

Two letters from teachers or counselors.

Personal Essay

500-650 word essay on a topic of your choice.

Application Fee

₱75 application fee (waiver available for qualifying students).

Additional Materials

Portfolio or audition required for some programs.

Financial Aid & Scholarships

We're committed to making education affordable for all qualified students

Merit Scholarships

Academic scholarships ranging from ₱5,000 to full tuition based on achievements.

Need-Based Aid

Financial aid packages based on family income and demonstrated need.

Work-Study Programs

Part-time employment opportunities on campus to help offset costs.

External Scholarships

Support in finding and applying for external scholarship opportunities.
